PROFOUNDGRID


A responsive grid system for fixed and fluid layouts.Built in SCSS,it gives you flexibility and full control.

Responsive

Make the grid fluid and add pre-media layouts as needed. Change the column and gutter widths and remove or add columns as needed.

Semantic

The grid keeps your markup semantic and works without any "grid_x" classes. Unless you want to use them, in which case they are included.

precise

Profound Grid uses negative marguins to calculate columns.Unlike with other grid systems. Fluid layouts will look exactly the same in every browser.
